Projects in Digital Composing Portfolio

Phase 1: Exploring Digital Media


Who am I?

In this project, I was becoming introduced to coding's very basic properties. I created a website introducing myself to the class and included some of my goals and interests.

The Art vs the Artist

Within this website is some of my favorite pieces of art. I struggled with some of the main properties of coding, such as creating texts to fit a certain size, but I did become more familiar with adding photos and creating buttons.

Testing buttons...

With my "branching" website, I experimented with buttons that take you through different paths. I stayed on the basic side of designing in order to focus my attention on getting the links right. I experimented with colors and fonts for the first time, and this porject will help me in the future to customize my websites more.

Phase 1 Course Goals


1. Foster the creative habits of mind

With Phase 1, I learned a lot of basics. I learned how to make a website and the further I got into the projects, the more I learned about making them your own. Putting a lot of my own art work into my projects made me think of future goals where I can create websites developing my creative skills as well as advertise myself and my art out into the real world.

2. Engage with others in a workshop environment.

During Phase 1, I struggled a lot. The process of code and building websites is something I have never experimented with. I found myself frusterated and lost in the beginning. But, throughout the sharing process with others in the classroom, I was able to learn some skills from my peers. They were able to help me troubleshoot some of my errors and alieviate some of my frusterations.

3. Think deeply about craft.

These projects were out of my comfort zone,but I pushed myself to learn the basic techniques and useful skills to have while creating in this class. I have put time and effort into creating these sites and had to really think about what I was going ot make and how I was going to do it. I used a lot of my own work as well as experimented with some new work to create my projects in Phase 1.

4. Engage in sustained research and skills development.

Throughout this experience, I have learned a lot. There was times where I stared at my computer and had no idea what I was doing. It took a lot of time for me to create even the most basic website, but I challenged myself to overcome the frusterations and try to keep learning. I used ChatGPT to help troubleshoot some of my code. I also rewatched the tutorials as many times as I could until I got some of the basics down. By the end of Phase 1 I was able to create something I wouldn't of been able to prior to this class.

5. Learn to present your work

Usually if I am not confident in my work, I tend to hide it. But in this class I realized it is all about growth and pushing boundaries. Sharing and presenting my work with others in the class has helped me learn some things from others as well as be more confident in the fact that I can only get better.
